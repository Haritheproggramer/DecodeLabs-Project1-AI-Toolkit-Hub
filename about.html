About

A small hub for finding useful AI tools

I built this to make discovering AI tools feel less like searching through random tabs and more like browsing a simple list that matches your goals.

What this is

A simple directory for real use

AI Toolkit Hub is a small collection of practical AI tools. The idea is straightforward: group tools by what they help you do (writing, coding, research, design, productivity, audio) and keep each page easy to scan.

Who it’s for

Students, creators, and developers

If you’re learning something new, working on a project, or just trying to get unstuck, you’ll likely find a tool here that matches your current workflow.

Why it exists

Less time hunting, more time doing

Instead of bouncing between websites, you can open a category and see what each tool is typically used for. The descriptions are meant to help you decide quickly.

How it helps

Clear summaries, not loud marketing

Each tool page explains what it does and when people usually use it, so you can pick the right one without guessing.

What I’m aiming for

A calm, student-friendly experience

I wanted the site to feel like something someone built thoughtfully—readable, organized, and focused on helping you move forward.
